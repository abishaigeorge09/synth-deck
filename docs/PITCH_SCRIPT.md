## Appendix: older product-section notes (optional)

### “Old product section”

SYNTH focuses on the three core workflows of a rowing coach.
- Drag-and-drop lineup builder that notifies athletes which boat they’re in for the session.
- Live session logger that tracks on-water pieces: distance, time, who is in which boat. Over time, provides individual performance progression. There is no way to automatically track on-water lineups currently.
- AI agent integrates with Google Sheets which allows coaches to take years of spreadsheet data into consideration when planning training schedules, and adjust on the go with fatigue trend visualisations.
